About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is an advanced phishing detection system that uses machine learning to identify suspicious emails, scam attempts, and phishing attacks.

How It Works

Our AI analyzes email content for common phishing indicators including:

🔗 Suspicious links & URLs
🏦 Brand name mentions
⏰ Urgency & pressure tactics
🔐 Verification requests
💰 Unrealistic money offers
⚠️ Account threat language

Technology

Built with Python, Scikit-learn, and NLTK. The system uses a Naive Bayes classifier trained on thousands of real spam and phishing emails, with TF-IDF vectorization for feature extraction.

Privacy

Your email content is not stored. Each analysis is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We never save or share your data.

Limitations

No system is perfect. Always use common sense when dealing with unexpected emails. When in doubt, contact the company directly through official channels, not through links in the email.
