About Email Guard

A simple tool to help you identify suspicious emails before you interact with them.

How it works

Email Guard analyzes email content using pattern recognition to flag potential spam or phishing attempts. It looks for common red flags like urgent language, suspicious links, and known spam patterns.

Accuracy

Based on tests with over 5,500 real emails, our system correctly identifies suspicious emails 97% of the time. It rarely flags legitimate emails as spam — about 0% false positive rate.

Privacy

Your email content is not stored. Each check is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

Limitations

No system is perfect. Always use your best judgment before clicking links or sharing personal information online.
